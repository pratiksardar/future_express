## 2. VIRAL MECHANICS TO BUILD INTO THE APP

### A. TikTok-Native Share Features

**1. "Share as Video Playcard" Button**
- One-tap export of article card as vertical video (9:16)
- Animated: Headline typewriter effect, odds counter spinning up
- Watermark: "thefutureexpress.com" + TikTok handle bottom
- Auto-saved to camera roll for immediate TikTok upload
- **Why It Works:** Removes friction from content creation

**2. Screenshot-Worthy Moment Generator**
- "Hot Take Card" generator: User picks article, app creates shareable graphic
- Retro newspaper style quote cards with contrarian takes
- Includes current odds + timestamp for credibility
- Built-in "Post to TikTok" button with pre-filled caption template
- **Why It Works:** Users become creators with zero effort

**3. "Duet Mode" Prediction Cards**
- Special article format designed for TikTok duets
- Left side: Just headline + odds (clean, readable)
- Right side: Empty space for user reaction
- Call to action: "Duet this if you agree/disagree"
- **Why It Works:** Invites participation, multiplies reach

### B. In-App Virality Triggers

**4. "Time Capsule" Feature**
- "Save your prediction" button that screenshots article + user's guess
- Auto-reminds user when market resolves (30 days later)
- Generates "I was right/wrong" share card
- Gamification: Prediction score leaderboard
- **Why It Works:** Creates return users + proof of accuracy content

**5. "Breaking News" Push Notifications**
- When odds swing >15% in 1 hour, push notification
- Notification text is TikTok-ready: "This went from 23% to 78% in ONE HOUR"
- Deep link to article with "Share the chaos" button
- **Why It Works:** Real-time content opportunities

**6. "Edition Drop" Countdown**
- Homepage shows countdown to next edition (every 4 hours)
- "Set Reminder" button that alerts 2 minutes before drop
- First to share new edition gets featured on app
- Creates appointment viewing behavior
- **Why It Works:** FOMO + consistency signals

### C. Social Proof Elements

**7. Live View Counter**
- "47,329 people reading this right now"
- Increases urgency, makes content feel important
- Shareable stat: "This prediction is going VIRAL"
- **Why It Works:** Herd mentality drives shares

**8. "Trending on TikTok" Badge**
- Auto-detect when article is being shared on TikTok
- Badge appears: "Viral on TikTok - 2.3M views"
- Clicking shows TikTok embed feed of videos about this article
- **Why It Works:** Platform cross-pollination

**9. Creator Attribution System**
- When creators make videos about articles, tag them in app
- "As seen in @username's TikTok" with embed
- Leaderboard of top creators driving traffic
- Incentivizes more creator content
- **Why It Works:** Relationship building + social proof

### D. Gamification Hooks

**10. "Prediction Streak" Counter**
- Track daily visits, award badges
- "You've checked tomorrow's news for 7 days straight"
- Shareable achievement cards
- **Why It Works:** Habit formation + flex content

**11. "Controversial Take of the Day"**
- Algorithm picks the most contrarian article daily
- "99% of people think X, but the data says Y"
- Badge: "CERTIFIED HOT TAKE"
- **Why It Works:** Debate drives comments/shares

**12. "Friend Challenge" Feature**
- "Challenge @friend to guess the odds"
- Both submit guesses, see who's closer to market
- Share results as TikTok video
- **Why It Works:** Social mechanics + competitive content
